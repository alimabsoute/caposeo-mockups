Honest Limits

What Content Editor doesn't do

TF-IDF coverage is a strong structural signal but not the only one that determines rankings. Backlink authority, domain trust, and EEAT signals aren't captured in a content editor. We don't claim that hitting a high score guarantees ranking improvement — we claim that you'll be structurally more similar to what's already ranking. For extremely low-competition queries (<100 monthly searches), the baseline may reflect thin existing content, reducing the signal quality. We flag this automatically.
